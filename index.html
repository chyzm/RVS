Retroviral Solutions specializes in the distribution of antiretroviral (ARV) medications and healthcare logistics. Our services include:

  • End-to-end ARV supply chain management
  • Last-mile delivery to healthcare facilities
  • Inventory management and stock monitoring
  • Emergency medication supply solutions
  • Technology-driven logistics optimization

We currently operate across all 36 states in Nigeria, with particular focus on underserved regions. Our distribution network includes:

  • Major urban centers (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, etc.)
  • Secondary cities and towns
  • Rural healthcare facilities through our last-mile partners
  • Special outreach programs for conflict-affected areas

We're continuously expanding our reach to ensure nationwide coverage.

Healthcare facilities can partner with us through several channels:

  1. Direct Supply Agreement: Register your facility to receive regular ARV supplies
  2. Emergency Stock Program: Access our emergency medication reserve during shortages
  3. Technology Integration: Connect your inventory systems with our monitoring platform
  4. Training Partnership: Participate in our healthcare provider training programs

Click here to submit a partnership request.

All medications distributed by Retroviral Solutions meet the highest international quality standards:

  • WHO prequalified or FDA-approved formulations
  • Sourced from certified manufacturers with GMP compliance
  • Stringent cold chain management where required
  • Batch-level traceability throughout our supply chain
  • Regular quality control testing

We maintain full documentation for all products and welcome quality audits from partner organizations.

Our multi-layered approach to prevent stockouts includes:

  • Strategic buffer stock at regional hubs
  • Diversified supplier network across multiple manufacturers
  • Real-time inventory monitoring across all partner facilities
  • Predictive analytics to anticipate demand surges
  • Emergency redistribution protocols

With shortage in ARV medication looming, we intend to fill this gap and maintain 98% availability for our partner facilities.
